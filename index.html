About DevGeek T-Shirts.

DevGeek T-shirts was an idea conceived after modest success on Kickstarter with a single t-shirt called The Super Geek Tee. After realizing there was an underserved market for developer themed t-shirts, I came up with a handful of design concepts, elicited help from a good friend to film a video, and launched DevGeek T-shirts on Kickstarter in April, 2013. About a month later, 469 backers made DevGeek T-shirts a success.
